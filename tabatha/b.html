Co-Design

Working through challenges — together

We bring people together to talk through real challenges and shape solutions as a group.

Instead of decisions being made in isolation, we create space for different perspectives to come forward — from teams, stakeholders, and the people directly impacted.

Through guided conversations and simple activities, we help groups:

  • Understand what's really going on
  • Hear perspectives that are often missed
  • Explore ideas openly before moving forward

The result is more grounded decisions and solutions people feel connected to.

Best for

  • Community and stakeholder engagement
  • Early-stage idea exploration
  • Building shared understanding
  • Navigating complex or sensitive topics

Game Development

Game-based experiences: thinking through complexity in a new way

We create simple, interactive games that help people explore real-world challenges in a hands-on way.

These experiences make it easier to:

  • See how decisions play out
  • Understand different perspectives
  • Talk about complex issues in a more open, less pressured way

Games create a shared experience — which often leads to more honest conversations and deeper insight.

Best for

  • Facilitating difficult or complex discussions
  • Learning through experience rather than presentation
  • Engaging groups that might not respond to traditional formats
  • Exploring "what could happen" before taking action

Custom Games

Why play?

Games are fun — but they can also get serious. Serious games create a safe space to foster empathy between players, test new ideas, and learn content in an engaging way. They use fun to motivate people to ask questions and explore topics that are often taboo. Custom-designed board and card games target the "pain points" as a deliberative dialogue tool, seeding a common lexicon and direction amongst players who are often unable to see past their own assumptions.

ReStrukt

Empowering your community

A suite of games focused on community engagement and exploring the complex and emotive relationships in a community faced with a challenge. ReStrukt creates space for participants to surface assumptions, test ideas, and build shared direction — even when views clash.

Shrimps Matter

Ocean ecosystems & interconnection

A game that demonstrates the interconnected and fragile nature of an ocean ecosystem. Current content is based around a Canadian Maritime ecosystem. Players discover how small changes ripple through the whole system — and why every species (yes, even shrimps) matters.

Can I Help?

Mental health & inclusion

A game that explores mental health and barriers to inclusion in a community by prompting conversations and empathy. Players can share lived experiences if they choose, creating a safe and supported space to explore topics that are often hard to talk about.
