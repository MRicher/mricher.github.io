The Chaos Cursor

A collaborative drawing game where everyone's mouse creates colourful trails. Work together to draw the challenge shape before time runs out!

How to Play

  • Each participant gets a unique colour trail
  • Move your mouse or finger across the canvas to draw
  • Work together to create the challenge shape in 30 seconds
  • Choose "New Challenge" to get a different shape to draw
  • Clear the canvas anytime to start fresh
