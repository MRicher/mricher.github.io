Games

Why play?

Games are fun — but they can also get serious. Serious games create a safe space to foster empathy between players, test new ideas, and learn content in an engaging way. They use fun to motivate people to ask questions and explore topics that are often taboo. Custom designed board and card games target the "pain points" as a deliberative dialogue tool, seeding a common lexicon and direction amongst players who are often unable to see past their own assumptions. Examples include ReStrukt (community engagement), Shrimps Matter (ocean ecosystems), and Can I Help? (mental health & inclusion).
