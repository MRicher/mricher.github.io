Custom Games

Why play?

Games are fun — but they can also get serious. Serious games create a safe space to foster empathy between players, test new ideas, and learn content in an engaging way. They use fun to motivate people to ask questions and explore topics that are often taboo. Custom designed board and card games target the "pain points" as a deliberative dialogue tool, seeding a common lexicon and direction amongst players who are often unable to see past their own assumptions.

ReStrukt

Empowering your community

A suite of games focused on community engagement and exploring the complex and emotive relationships in a community faced with a challenge. ReStrukt creates space for participants to surface assumptions, test ideas, and build shared direction — even when views clash.

Shrimps Matter

Ocean ecosystems & interconnection

A game that demonstrates the interconnected and fragile nature of an ocean ecosystem. Current content is based around a Canadian Maritime ecosystem. Players discover how small changes ripple through the whole system — and why every species (yes, even shrimps) matters.

Can I Help?

Mental health & inclusion

A game that explores mental health and barriers to inclusion in a community by prompting conversations and empathy. Players can share lived experiences if they choose, creating a safe and supported space to explore topics that are often hard to talk about.
