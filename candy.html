Our Sweet Journey

1995 - The Beginning

Founded by Maria and Giuseppe Dolce in Toronto, starting with traditional Italian confections passed down through generations.

2005 - Expansion

Opened our first retail location in downtown Toronto, becoming a beloved destination for artisanal sweets.

2015 - Digital Innovation

Launched our e-commerce platform, bringing Sweet Dreams to customers across Canada with secure online ordering.

2025 - Sustainable Future

Committed to eco-friendly packaging and supporting local suppliers while maintaining our artisanal quality standards.

Our Mission
